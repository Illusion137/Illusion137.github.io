Desaturated dark blue limestone walls imprisoned all of the Noven people. There was neither a ceiling nor a floor. Plummeting, all plummeting one-thousand kilometers below, onto a mountain of corpses that layer the bottom of the first of an unknown amount of layers of Illusion’s Abyss. 

A white-haired boy, reaching the end of his plummet, on fire from velocity he was traveling down, like a meteoroid blazing through earth’s atmosphere. The boy waved his hand around to grab onto the fossil gray shirt of a nearby person. Upon grabbing ahold of the fossil gray shirt he tore off a long piece of fabric and then began to tie it around his eyes, once done he uncovered only his left eye from the fabric.

The boy tensed every muscle within his body, following, he whispered the word he desired the most at that moment.

“Indomitable”

Immediately after he crashed into the mountain of corpses, using them as a cushion for his fall. Many corpses became soaring tens of meters in the air, just for them to re-crash back down onto the boy. 

Lying his back, his entire body feeling numb with paresthesia, unable to move a muscle, as the bodies on top of him rolled off of him. The boy observed as the agglomeration of Noven plummeting from up above would spew out all their flesh and blood next to him on striking the ground. As he watched this foul display of carnage it felt like time slowed down, he was able to observe all the details of everyone before they hit the corpse mountain below.

Their pupils dilated, the heavy breathing shown in their chest, their sweat and tears cohering together, the screams that echoed throughout the walls of the abyss, families clinging to each other praying for their safety. These were a first for most that were falling.

Time passed along with the rain of people from above; time entered a brief hiatus. The white-haired boy perceived this anomaly, yet was incapable of understanding what just happened.
